Association Rule Mining algorithm (Apriori) :

    • Load the .arff/.csv file.
    • Go to Preprocess -> Associate -> Choose -> associations -> Apriori
    • Invoke clustering by clicking “start” button
    • Result list is generated (rules are generated)
    • (Optional) You can change metrixType(confidence), lowerbound-min-support etc...
      by left click on Choose(Apriori), Click Save
    • Right click on Result list then click Visualize tree.
    • Right click on Result list then click Visualize cluster assignment.
